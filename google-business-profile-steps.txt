Google Business Profile Setup (Hassaan Bashir)

What you need (prepare first)
- Business / profile name (example: "Hassaan Bashir - Medical Imaging Technologist")
- Category (example: r"Medical diagnostic imaging cente" or "Medical clinic")
- Service area: Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan
- Phone number
- Website: https://hassaanbashir.tech
- Short business description (750 chars max)
- Profile photo + cover photo
- Working hours

Step-by-step
1) Go to Google Business Profile and sign in with your Google account.
2) Click "Manage now" and enter your profile name.
3) Choose the best category.
4) Choose whether customers can visit a location:
   - If you do not see clients at a fixed address, choose "No" and set service area.
5) Add your phone and website.
6) Verify your profile (usually by phone, email, or video).
7) Add hours, services, and a short description.
8) Upload your logo, profile photo, and cover image.
9) Publish your profile and keep it updated.

Tips to help Knowledge Panel
- Use the same name, title, and photo as your website and LinkedIn.
- Add services and make a short, clear description.
- Keep contact info consistent everywhere.
